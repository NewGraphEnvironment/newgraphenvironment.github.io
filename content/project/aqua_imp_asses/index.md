## Sheep Creek Biological Impact Assessment
Environmental impact assessment of landfill leachate on aquatic values in major tributary to the Salmo River. Water quality impacts quantified using analysis of benthic macro-invertebrate and periphyton communities.  Fieldwork, analysis and reporting completed.
