Open-Source Watershed Science

Who We Are

We are biologists and programmers working at the intersection of aquatic ecosystem restoration, spatial data science, and open-source tool development. We build productive relationships between First Nations, regulators, non-profits, proponents, scientists, and stewardship groups — with an emphasis on inclusive engagement and knowledge sharing.

Our work spans field assessment, watershed-scale analysis, and dynamic reporting. We develop and maintain an integrated framework connecting field data collection, spatial databases, habitat modelling, imagery pipelines, and reproducible reporting — all open source, all publicly available.
